## A Journey That Began With Questions

In 2014, a family of lifelong learners — Archana, Manish, Maitreyee and Atri — made a life-changing decision. Having spent over two decades in corporate America working with top companies like Oracle, IBM, and Infosys, they chose to return to Bharat and embark on a radically different path.

The seeds of Asmakam were planted in endless questions: *How is every action and milestone in our life dictated by society's conditioned belief system? What are the false definitions of growth, success and happiness that we've been living by?*

## From Questions to Action

What started as personal experiments with sustainable living, natural farming, forestation, and water conservation gradually evolved into something much larger — a vision for reimagining education itself.

We began practicing **zero outsourcing for education, food and health**. This wasn't just a philosophy; it became our way of life. Growing our own food, building with natural materials, and learning from the land around us.

## The Birth of Asmakam

**Asmakam** — meaning "OURS" in Sanskrit — captures the essence of our vision. This isn't one person's or one family's initiative. It belongs to all of us.

Our Life University stands on three pillars:

1. **Sustainability** — experiencing natural living, food forests, and regenerative practices
2. **Wellness** — holistic well-being through food as medicine, yoga, naturopathy, and more
3. **Lifelong Learning** — hands-on programs that develop creativity, life skills, and purpose

## A Campus in the Food Forest

Our upcoming campus in 15 acres of natural food forest in Indore is our labor of love. Using ancient architectural techniques of lime and stone, we're building a space that embodies our values — a natural residential space with an amphitheater, community center, natural pond, and camping site.

## Join Our Journey

We invite learners, parents, educators, and anyone seeking a more meaningful life to connect with us. Whether through our residential programs, retreats, summer experiences, or online offerings, there's a place for you at Asmakam.
