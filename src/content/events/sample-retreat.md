## About This Retreat

Join us for a transformative 3-day retreat at our food forest campus in Indore. This immersive experience brings together the pillars of sustainability and wellness in a natural setting.

### What You'll Experience

- **Natural Farming**: Hands-on experience with permaculture and food forest practices
- **Wellness Sessions**: Yoga, meditation, and naturopathy practices
- **Plant-Based Nutrition**: Learn about food as medicine and PBWF diet lifestyle
- **Natural Building**: Tour our eco-natural house built with lime and stone
- **Community Living**: Experience the joy of simple, conscious living

### Who Is This For?

This retreat welcomes individuals and families who are curious about ecological living, sustainable practices, and holistic wellness. No prior experience is needed — just an open mind and willingness to learn.

### Accommodation

Participants will stay in our natural residential space within the food forest. All meals are plant-based, prepared with ingredients from our own farm.

Contact us to reserve your spot.
